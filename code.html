About us

We create smooth and elegant spaces based on understanding the client's aspirations, the importance of the location and maximizing the potential of the elements in the space, then developing this with imagination and vision (3D images). The result is an interior design that tells a story in relation to its location, taking into account the four walls in which it is located, and most importantly, at its core, reflects the client's individual personality... Successful interior design should enhance the way you live, not dictate it.

What We Do

Housedeco is a company that produces highly qualified furniture and interior items.Founded in the Netherlands 1982.Production was moved to Turkey in 1992.The strong workmanship and qualitative and aesthetic modeling are highly valued by local customers.

Our Goal

Housedeco designs not only the furniture but also the floor, walls, ceiling, doors, windows, lighting and all accessories. Housedeco consult the entire project from start to finish. Our experienced interior architects are at all stages of the project. Housedeco manufactures everything in hir own factory: tables, chairs, sideboards, consoles, coffee tables, chesterfield sofas, contemporary seating, beds, wardrobes, nightstands, make-up tables.
